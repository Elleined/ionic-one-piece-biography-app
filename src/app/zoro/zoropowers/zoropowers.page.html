Power & Abilities Main Swords Wado Ichimonji One of the 21 Great Grade Swords. It was forged by Shimotsuki Kozaburo, the same swordsmith who forged Enma. Enma One of the 21 Great Great Swords that belonged to the famous samurai and pirate, Kozuki Oden who passed it down to his daughter, Hiyori, who gifted it for Zoro in return for Shushi. It is infamous for drawing out and draining all the Ryuo (Haki) of its owner. It is the widely known as a sword that can hurt Kaido. Sandai Kitetsu The Sandai Kitetsu is one of the Grade swords. Like all of its predecessor Kitetsu swords, this one is said to be a "Cursed Sword/Blade". It is one of Kozuki Sukiyaki's creations. Sword Techniques Santoryu (Three Sword Style) Nitoryu (Two Sword Style) Ittoryu (One Sword Style) Kyutoryu (Nine Sword Style) Haki Conqueror's Haki Zoro managed to use Conqueror's Haki somehow, but he isn't aware of this power dwelling within him yet. Armament Haki Roronoa Zoro was trained by Dracule Mihawk, the strongest swordsman in One Piece, during the two-year timeskip. The training focused quite a lot on Armament Haki, which is why Zoro's usage of this power is so refined at the moment. He can coat his sword with armament haki making it black sword. Observation Haki Zoro also managed to learn how to use Observation Haki, although his usage of it isn't nearly as good.
