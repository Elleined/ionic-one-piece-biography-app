 Biography Roronoa Zoro is a swordsman and the combatant of the Straw Hat Pirates. Also known as "Pirate Hunter Zoro". He is one of the most versatile fighters in the series and one of the strongest four fighters in the Straw Hat Pirates. He is also the most loyal crewmate to Luffy amongst the Straw Hats. Zoro and Kuina making their promise to become the world's greatest swordsmen. As a child, Zoro trained at a dojo in Shimotsuki Village. Zoro stated in the Baratie, that, "Once he decided that he would become an invincible swordsman, he left his life behind".
