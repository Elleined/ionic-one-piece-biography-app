Powers & Abilities Fish-Man Karate Fish-Man Karate has a number of devastating underwater moves designed to suit the enhanced strength and speed that Fish-Man possess while in their natural environment. Fish-Man Jujutsu Fish-Man Jujutsu is a martial art that is practiced by the Fish-Man race, alongside Fish-Man Karate. It is a style that revolves around grappling and throw techniques.
