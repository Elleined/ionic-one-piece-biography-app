 Biography Jinbe, also known as The Knight of the Sea, is one of the main characters in the anime and manga series, One Piece. He's a whale shark Fishman, and a master of both Fishman Karate and Fishman Jujutsu. And the helmsman of the Straw Hat Pirates and one of the Senior Officers of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet. Jinbe grew up as an orphan in the orphanage that became the Fishman District, alongside Arlong, Macro, and Fisher Tiger. He trained at the Fishman Karate Dojo at an early age and earned a black belt in Fishman Karate while still in his childhood. Around sixteen years before the current storyline, Jinbe was a soldier of the Neptune Army.
